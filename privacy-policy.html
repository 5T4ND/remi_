Privacy Policy for Tendly

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tendly reminder app ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information stored on your device

Tendly stores all of your reminders, categories, notes, completion history and settings locally on your device using a private on-device database. We do not have an account system and we do not upload, sync, or transmit your reminders to any server we control. If you uninstall the App, this data is removed from your device.

2. Advertising

Tendly displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process certain information, including your device's Advertising ID, approximate or coarse information derived from your device, and ad-interaction data. This processing is performed by Google, not by us.

You can learn how Google uses this data here: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

3. Permissions we request

  • Notifications — to alert you when a reminder is due.
  • Alarms & reminders (exact alarm) — to deliver reminders at the exact time you set.
  • Run at startup — to re-schedule your reminders after the device restarts.
  • Internet / network state — used only to load advertisements.

4. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or the content of your reminders.

5. Children

Tendly is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
